Japanese Katsu
Chicken

Who can deny the allure of crispy, juicy fried chicken? Not me!
Not when I can make them at home in approximately 30 minutes.
I'm referring to this Chicken Katsu (チキンカツ),
a Japanese variant of chicken schnitzel or chicken tenders.

Ingredients:

Directions:

  • Add salt and pepper to the chicken breasts' both surfaces
  • Make separate shallow plates for the flour, beaten egg, and panko crumbs.
  • Cover well chicken breast on both sides with flour, dip them afterwards in egg, then press into panko crumbs.
  • Using medium-high heat, warm the oil in a big skillet.
  • Fry the chicken for three to four minutes on each side until golden brown.
  • Drain by moving to a plate lined with paper towels
